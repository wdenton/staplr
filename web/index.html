STAPLR (Sounds in Time Actively Performing Library Reference)
turns the reference desks at York University Libraries into music.

What you hear just happened.

STAPLR is built with Sonic Pi by William Denton, a librarian at York University.

For background, see this article from Open Shelf (February 2016).
